# TKD Top Trumps — Design Ideas

## Approach 1: Dark Dojo Warrior
<response>
<idea>
**Design Movement:** Neo-Brutalism meets Martial Arts Aesthetic
**Core Principles:** Raw power, high contrast, bold typography, physical card feel
**Color Philosophy:** Deep obsidian black (#0A0A0F) with electric crimson (#E8001D) accents — the colours of a black belt. Gold (#FFD700) for stat highlights. Creates tension, danger, and prestige.
**Layout Paradigm:** Full-bleed card face takes 80% of viewport. Stats appear as vertical bars on the right side. Navigation is minimal — swipe/arrow to move between cards.
**Signature Elements:** Red diagonal slash motif across card corners; brushstroke texture on card backgrounds; Korean hangul watermark text
**Interaction Philosophy:** Cards "slam" into view with a physical impact animation. Stat bars fill with energy on reveal.
**Animation:** Card flip with 3D perspective; stat bars fill left-to-right with a power surge effect; win/lose screen with shockwave ripple
**Typography System:** "Black Han Sans" for headings (bold Korean-inspired), "Rajdhani" for stats and numbers
</idea>
<text>Dark Dojo Warrior — obsidian black with electric crimson and gold, physical card slam animations, Korean-inspired typography</text>

</response>

## Approach 2: Championship Gold
<response>
<idea>
**Design Movement:** Sports Trading Card Collector — Premium Foil Edition
**Core Principles:** Prestige, collectibility, metallic sheen, tournament energy
**Color Philosophy:** Deep navy (#0D1B2A) base with championship gold (#C9A84C) and platinum silver (#E8E8E8). Feels like a trophy cabinet.
**Layout Paradigm:** Card grid on the left, active card detail on the right (split-screen). Mobile: full-screen card with swipe gestures.
**Signature Elements:** Holographic shimmer effect on card surface; embossed belt rank badge; taekwondo silhouette watermark
**Interaction Philosophy:** Hovering a card lifts it with a foil shimmer. Selecting a category triggers a dramatic comparison reveal.
**Animation:** Card lift with drop shadow on hover; stat number count-up animation; victory confetti burst
**Typography System:** "Bebas Neue" for names and stats, "Lato" for body text
</idea>
<text>Championship Gold — deep navy with gold foil shimmer, collectible card aesthetic, dramatic stat comparison reveals</text>
<probability>0.08</probability>
</response>

## Approach 3: Neon Dojo
<response>
<idea>
**Design Movement:** Cyberpunk Martial Arts — Digital Dojo
**Core Principles:** Energy, speed, digital power, futuristic training
**Color Philosophy:** Near-black (#080C14) with neon teal (#00F5D4) and hot magenta (#FF006E). Feels like a holographic training simulation.
**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric — card on the left floats at an angle; stats panel on the right uses horizontal progress bars with glow effects. Background has subtle animated particle grid.
**Signature Elements:** Neon glow borders on cards; scanline texture overlay; hexagonal stat radar chart
**Interaction Philosophy:** Everything glows on interaction. Stats pulse when selected. Card selection triggers a digital "scan" animation.
**Animation:** Card scan-in from top with glitch effect; stats reveal with neon fill animation; battle result with screen flash
**Typography System:** "Orbitron" for headings and stats, "Space Grotesk" for body
</idea>
<text>Neon Dojo — cyberpunk dark with neon teal/magenta, holographic card effects, digital scan animations</text>
<probability>0.06</probability>
</response>
